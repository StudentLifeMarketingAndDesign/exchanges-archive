Jennifer Croft

Jennifer Croft is currently completing a PhD in Comparative Literary Studies at Northwestern University, where she is working on twentieth-century Central European and Latin American fiction. Her translations from Polish have appeared in World Literature Today, Words Without Borders, Absinthe, and others.
