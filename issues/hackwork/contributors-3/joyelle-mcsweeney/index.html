Hackwork » Contributors » Joyelle McSweeney

Joyelle McSweeney is the author of the poetry books The Red Bird and The Commandrine, both from Fence, and of the novels Flet (Fence, 2007) and Nylund, the Sarcographer (Tarpaulin Sky Press, 2007). With Johannes Goransson, she publishes Action Books and Action, Yes, a press and web journal for international writings and hybrid forms. She teaches in the MFA program at the University of Notre Dame. Excerpts from this translation have appeared in such journals as American Poetry Review and Conduit.
