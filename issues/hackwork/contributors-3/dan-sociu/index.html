Hackwork » Contributors » Dan Sociu

Dan Sociu was born in 1978 in Botosani, Romania. He belongs to the younger generation of poets, the so-called poets of 2000, who are often called representatives of "Miserabilism" by Romanian literary critics. A selection of his poems has been translated for English anthologies: New European Poets (Graywolf 2008), No Longer Poetry (Heaventree Press, ed. David Morely, 2007). Urbancholia (Polirom, 2008) was his debut as a novelist. His second novel Special Needs (Polirom, 2008) has further established his standing among contemporary Romanian writers.
