Carole Viers

Carole Viers is a lecturer of Comparative Literature at the University of California, Los Angeles and at California State University, Long Beach. She holds a Ph.D. in Comparative Literature from the University of California, Los Angeles. Her areas of interest include translation studies, philosophies of aesthetics, and Italian and French literature of the twentieth century. She served as president of Babel, a study group dedicated to the practice and study of translation, throughout her studies at UCLA. In 2004 she was a principal organizer of an international workshop for graduate student translators, which has become a biennial conference, rotating among UCLA, the University of Iowa, and Columbia University.
