Ryan Gallagher

Ryan Gallagher lives in Lowell, MA with his wife, daughter, and son. He is co-founder of Bootstrap Press with Derek Fenner and is the author of Plum Smash and Other Flashbulbs (poems), published in 2005, and The Complete Poems of Gaius Valerius Catullus (translations), published in 2008.
