Julien Friedler

Born in 1950 in Belgium, Julien Friedler spent most of his adult life working as a psychoanalyst and writer. Only later, in 1997, did he discover his voice as an artist through work that interrogates modernity with all of its shortcomings and future prospects. As part of his psychoanalytical background, Friedler was analyzed by the prominent French psychoanalyst, Jacques Lacan, and in 1994 founded his own research center, La Moire, to promote a multidisciplinary approach to the psyche based on an interrogation of Freudian texts. In 2005 Friedler closed the research center to concentrate exclusively on the plastic arts.
